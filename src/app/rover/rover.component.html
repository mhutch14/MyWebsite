Experience

Every since about two years ago I discovered one of my passions, and that passion was caring for any and all types of dogs at my local animal shelter. Being a student I spent all my breaks and everyday I had free doing this. Now that I am graduated I am working a full time job as a controls engineer and would love to in the free time help others with care of there animals. I still volunteer whenever I can at my local shelter and when I discovered this service I just knew that I had to become apart of it. Although I do not have a ton of time during the week due to my full time job I would love to help after work and on the weekends as much as possible.

When Watching Your Pet

I am willing to take your dog on a short simple walk in your neighborhood, or go for a much longer walk on trails or at parks. I have learned a bit about training over my years of experience at the shelter and will attempt to work on basic commands with your dog, if we are in an environment that allows.

What I Would Like to Know

I just like to know how your dog is with other people and other dogs. My favorite breeds include huskies, retrievers, and German shepherds, however I am more than happy with all types of dogs.
